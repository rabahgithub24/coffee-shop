About Our Coffee

Welcome to our cozy corner of coffee heaven! Established in 2010, we've been serving premium, ethically sourced coffee to our community. Our master baristas carefully select and roast each bean to perfection, ensuring every cup tells a story of quality and passion.

Nestled in the heart of Miami, Florida, our coffee shop brings warmth and character to the vibrant Brickell neighborhood. With our outdoor seating area surrounded by lush tropical plants and the gentle Miami breeze, we've created the perfect spot for both locals and visitors to enjoy their daily coffee ritual. Our prime location, just steps away from Brickell City Centre, makes us the ideal destination for morning commuters, business meetings, or casual afternoon breaks.
